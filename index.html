About Us

MYBC is a youth-driven non-profit company empowering young people in Mpumalanga through entrepreneurship, skills development, and business education. We aim to connect youth with opportunities, mentors, and resources that unlock their full potential.

Our Programs

  • Business Skills Bootcamps
  • Entrepreneurship Development Incubator
  • Youth in Tech & Innovation Hub
  • Market Access Dialogues
  • Community Upliftment Initiatives
